About

RAYS is the Annual Cultural, Sports and Technical Festival of Rizvi College of Engineering of Rizvi Education Society which is a Public Charitable Trust established under The Societies Act 1950 and registered under the provisions of The Bombay Public Act, 1950. The main objective of the Society has always been promoting education at all levels. Rizvi Education Society began with the college of Arts, Science and Commerce and Rizvi High School in the year 1985. It later spread its wings and branched into Rizvi College of Architecture, Rizvi College of Hotel Management & Catering Technology, Rizvi College of Engineering, Rizvi Institute of Management Studies & Research, Rizvi Law College and Rizvi College of Education. Apart from the Institutions in Mumbai, Rizvi Education Society also manages several schools & Colleges in Jaunpur, Allahabad and Lucknow. Rizvi Education Society also has plans to establish Degree and Diploma Colleges in the areas of Pharmacy, Journalism and Medical Sciences. Rizvi Education Society emphasizes on academic excellence with equal importance for co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, which have been given their due importance as it plays a significant role in the lives of the students. In a span of a few years, Rizvi Education Society has given a lot of talent to this sports loving city. Some of the Rizviites are proud members of the Ranji Trophy Squad. To sum it up, Rizvi Education Society, with its concept of “pre-primary education to exemplary career” is acting as a catalyst to metamorphise a learning society.
